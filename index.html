How to Clue:

  • Type a 5-letter word and press ENTER (or click LOCK IN) to submit your guess.
  • Tiles change color to reveal clues:
    G Correct letter & spot. Y In word, wrong spot. X Not in word.
  • You have a 60-second timer that ticks down.
  • Every correct (green) letter you guess inflicts damage on your opponent, cutting 3 seconds off their timer!
  • If you solve the word, you stop your countdown and deal massive timer damage.

Opponent Intel:

On the right side of the screen, you can see the **Opponent Intel** board. It shows their active board progress in real-time, letting you know how close they are to solving the word.
